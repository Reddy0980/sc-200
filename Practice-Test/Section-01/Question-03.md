## Question
You have been hired as a security expert for Microsoft 365 suite. When mitigating incidents with Microsoft 365 Defender, what are the needed steps to investigate an incident in the Microsoft Security Center portal? 

## Options
Option 1 : Reviewing alerts, investigating the incident, determining root cause, and taking corrective action 
Option 2 : Taking corrective action, determining root cause, reviewing alerts, and investigating the incident
Option 3 : Investigating the incident, reviewing alerts, taking corrective action, and determining the root cause  
Option 4 : Determining root cause, reviewing alerts, investigating the incident, and taking corrective action
Option 5 : Investigating the incident, taking corrective action, determining the root cause, and reviewing alerts
Option 6 : Reviewing alerts, taking corrective action, investigating the incident, and determining the root cause

## Answers
Option 3 :  12

## Reference Links
https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/modules/mitigate-incidents-microsoft-365-defender/3-manage-incidents 
 
## Explanation
When mitigating incidents with Microsoft 365 Defender, investigating the incident is the first step that should be taken. After investigating, the security team should review alerts related to the incident and then take corrective actions. Finally, the root cause should be determined to prevent the incident from happening again. Option c is the correct answer as it follows the correct sequence of steps. Options a, b, d, e, and f have the incorrect order of steps.
